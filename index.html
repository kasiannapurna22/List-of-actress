The Best Actress

  1. Alia Bhatt

    2. Born on March 23, 1993, Alia Bhatt is an Indian actor who made her debut with Karan Johar’s directorial Student Of The Year, which released in 2012. She first appeared as a child artist in Saanransh (1999), directed by her father and ace director Mahesh Bhatt. She is a step-sister to actor and filmmaker Pooja Bhatt. The actor rose to fame with her portrayal in the road drama Highway (2014), which won her a Filmfare Critics Award for Best Actress, and the crime drama Udta Punjab (2016). Alia made her singing debut with her comedy-romance film, Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania. She is said to be dating her Student of The Year co-star Sidharth Malhotra. The two even shared the screen space in Shakun Batra’s Kapoor & Sons.


  2. Anushka Shetty

    3. Anushka Shetty (born 7 November 1981) is an Indian actress, model and South Indian film star from Karnataka, India. She is known for her paintings in Tamil and Telugu films. The superstar previously worked as a yoga instructor. In the year 2005, Shetty made her debut in Telugu cinema with 'Super'. Anushka made her acting debut with Puri Jagannath's Telugu film Super in 2005. Nagarjuna and Ayesha Takia were also working with her in this film. In the same year, she also acted in the second film "Maha Nandi". Srihari and Sumant were with her in this film.


  3. Mrunal Thakur

    4. Mrunal Thakur is an Indian film actress, who has worked predominantly in Bollywood and Hindi television. Mrunal has worked in popular movies like Love Sonia, Pooja Meri Jaan, Pippa, and Sita Ramam. Mrunal's previous film to hit the theatres was Pooja Meri Jaan in the year 2022. Mrunal earned huge critical and commercial success with the biographical films Super 30 and Batla House. She made her Telugu film debut with Sita Ramam (2022), which received huge appreciation from the audience.


  4. Ritu Varma

    5. Ritu Varma is an Indian film actress from Telangana who mainly works in Telugu cinema. She is best known for her performance in the award-winning short film Anukokunda and the movie Pelli Choopulu, which elevated her into overnight fame.


  5. Kalyani Priyadarshan

Kalyani Priyadarshan (born 5 April 1993) is an Indian actress who predominantly appears in Malayalam, Tamil and Telugu films. She began her film career as an assistant production designer, and made her acting debut in the Telugu film Hello (2017). She won the Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut and the SIIMA Award for Best Telugu Female Debut for her performance.
